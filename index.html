What Do We Do?

Food is a finite resource and bees are a key to survival our long-term survival as a species. Although no one source for their widespread disappearance has been identified, the danger in real and imminent. If there are no bees to pollinate the food bearing plants and trees, our food sources and options diminish substantially, which would lead to pockets of starvation throughout the world and a necessary move towards more GMO foods in order to feed the population.
